Example Prompts for Browser Automation with Playwright MCP
===========================================================

NEWS & INFORMATION
------------------
1. "Go to Google News and find the top 5 headlines about AI"
2. "Navigate to BBC News and get the latest technology news"
3. "Search Google for 'breaking news today' and summarize what you find"
4. "Go to Reuters and find the top business news"
5. "Visit TechCrunch and get the latest startup news"

MOVIES & ENTERTAINMENT
---------------------
1. "Find movies playing near me in [Your City]"
2. "Go to Fandango and check what movies are showing this weekend"
3. "Search Google for 'movies in theaters now' and list the results"
4. "Navigate to IMDb and find the top rated movies of 2024"
5. "Go to Rotten Tomatoes and check the scores for [Movie Name]"

SHOPPING & PRICE CHECKS
-----------------------
1. "Go to Amazon and search for wireless headphones under $100"
2. "Navigate to eBay and find the current price of Nintendo Switch"
3. "Check the price of MacBook Air on Best Buy"
4. "Go to Target and search for winter jackets on sale"
5. "Find the best deals on [Product] across different websites"

WEATHER & LOCATION
------------------
1. "Go to weather.com and get the 7-day forecast for [City]"
2. "Navigate to Weather.gov and check if it will rain tomorrow"
3. "Search Google Maps for coffee shops near [Address]"
4. "Find the weather forecast for this weekend"

RESEARCH & LEARNING
------------------
1. "Go to Wikipedia and get the summary of 'Quantum Computing'"
2. "Navigate to Stack Overflow and search for Python async/await examples"
3. "Go to GitHub and find trending repositories this week"
4. "Search for 'machine learning tutorials' on YouTube"
5. "Visit MDN Web Docs and find the documentation for JavaScript Promises"

WEB SCRAPING & DATA EXTRACTION
------------------------------
1. "Go to this website [URL] and extract all the product titles"
2. "Navigate to [URL] and get the contact information"
3. "Scrape the table data from this page [URL]"
4. "Get all the links from the navigation menu on [URL]"
5. "Extract the meta description and title from [URL]"

FORM AUTOMATION (Use with test data only!)
------------------------------------------
1. "Fill out the newsletter signup form on [URL] with test@example.com"
2. "Navigate to this contact form and fill it with test data"
3. "Search for 'Python programming' on Google"
4. "Go to YouTube and search for 'React tutorials'"

SCREENSHOTS & MONITORING
------------------------
1. "Take a screenshot of google.com homepage"
2. "Navigate to [URL] and take a screenshot of the pricing section"
3. "Go to this website and capture the full page screenshot"
4. "Take screenshots of the top 3 search results for [Query]"

BROWSER INTERACTIONS
-------------------
1. "Go to Twitter, search for #AI, and show me the top tweets"
2. "Navigate through the pages of [URL] and collect all article titles"
3. "Click on the first search result for [Query] on Google"
4. "Scroll down on [URL] and load more content"

TESTING & DEBUGGING
------------------
1. "Check if [URL] loads without errors"
2. "Navigate to [URL] and check the console for any errors"
3. "Test if the search functionality works on [URL]"
4. "Monitor network requests when loading [URL]"

COMPLEX WORKFLOWS
----------------
1. "Go to Google, search for 'best laptops 2024', visit the first 3 results, and compare the recommendations"
2. "Find movie times for [Movie] near me, check reviews on Rotten Tomatoes, and take a screenshot"
3. "Search for 'pizza restaurants near [Location]', get the top 5 results with ratings"
4. "Go to Amazon, search for [Product], filter by 4+ stars, and list the top 3 results with prices"

==========================================================================
TIPS FOR BEST RESULTS:
==========================================================================

1. Be specific about what you want extracted or automated
2. Provide URLs when possible
3. Use test/dummy data for any form filling
4. Ask for screenshots if you want visual confirmation
5. You can chain multiple actions together in one prompt
6. The agent will ask for permission before using browser tools

EXAMPLE CONVERSATION:
--------------------
You: "Go to Google and find top news about space exploration"
Agent: [Requests permission to use browser tools]
You: [Grant permission]
Agent: [Navigates to Google, searches, returns results]

You: "Now take a screenshot of the NASA website"
Agent: [Navigates to NASA, takes screenshot]

You: "What were the console errors on that page?"
Agent: [Returns console messages from the NASA page]
